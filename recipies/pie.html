pie

Description

yummy pie that is easy to make and flavorful

ingredients

  • flour
  • oil
  • fruit of choice
  • sugar
  1. make the dough for the pie with the flour
  2. roll out pie dough and place in pan
  3. put fruit in pan on top of bottom crust, then top with another pie crust
  4. bake at 350 for 25 minutes
